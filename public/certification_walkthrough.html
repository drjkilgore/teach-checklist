Two Ways to Complete

You have
two options
for Stage 2.

📧 Option A — Email Link

  • Receive an email from the system when a coach submits
  • Click "Complete Your Portion" button
  • Opens directly in your browser
  • No login or account needed
  • Link expires after 30 days

🖥️ Option B — Admin Portal

  • Log in at /admin with your credentials
  • Find submissions showing ⏳ Awaiting Advisor
  • Click ✏️ Complete Stage 2 button
  • Full checklist opens in a modal
  • Best if you check the portal regularly
Either way, same experience
Whether you use the email link or the Admin Portal, you see the same checklist — the coach's items pre-filled for reference, and your items clearly labeled YOUR ITEM.

After You Submit

What happens
after you submit.

Once you click Submit Final Checklist, the system handles everything automatically.

Final PDF generated instantly
PDFShift renders a clean, branded PDF of the fully completed checklist.
PDF emailed to the coach
The coach receives the final checklist in their inbox automatically — no action needed from you.
Confirmation copy sent to you
You receive a CC of the final email as your confirmation.
Record updated to "Fully Complete"
The submission in the Admin Portal updates to ✓ Complete status.
All files stored permanently
The PDF and all uploaded documents are saved in the secure document repository — accessible forever from the Admin Portal.
🎉
You're ready

That's your
whole role.

Wait for the email, click the link (or use the Admin Portal), check your items, and submit. The system does the rest — PDF generation, notifications, storage, everything.

One important note
Each email link is unique to one resident and expires after 30 days. If a link has expired, ask the coach to resubmit. If you have Admin Portal access, you can always complete it from there instead.
