#TEACH Resident Completion Checklist — Guide

What This App Does

This tool lets coaches document and submit a resident's program completion requirements. It generates a state-specific checklist, delivers it by email as a formatted PDF, and keeps a permanent record of all submissions.

How to Complete a Checklist

  1. 1
    Resident Info — Enter the resident's name, Student ID, and the recipient email. Student ID is required to save and resume drafts.
  2. 2
    Checklist — Check each completed item. Use Save Draft to save progress and come back later. The resident can resume using their Student ID.
  3. 3
    Documents — Attach supporting files (transcripts, evaluations, certifications). All files are included in the email.
  4. 4
    Submit — Review and submit. A PDF checklist is auto-generated and emailed to the recipient with you CC'd.

Saving & Resuming Drafts

Click Save Draft on the checklist step at any time. The draft is tied to the resident's Student ID. To resume, enter the Student ID in the "Resume a saved draft" box on Step 1.

Drafts are saved indefinitely until the checklist is submitted. Admins and coaches can see all open drafts in the Admin Panel.

Admin Panel

Go to /admin on this site to manage submissions, view open drafts, manage coach accounts, and download Excel reports. Your access is limited to your assigned states.
