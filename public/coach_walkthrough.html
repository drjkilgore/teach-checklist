How It Works

A two-stage
process.

The checklist is split between you and the certification team. Each party only sees and completes their own items.

📋 Stage 1 — You

  • Section 1 — All coursework items
  • Section 2 — All #TEACH assessments
  • Section 4 — Final coaching conversation
  • Upload supporting documents
  • Submit → certification team is notified

✅ Stage 2 — Certification Team

  • State assessments & requirements
  • Teaching hours & documentation
  • Financial standing, transcripts
  • Background checks, letters
  • Final PDF generated → emailed to you
You will know when it's done
Once the certification team completes Stage 2, a final PDF is automatically emailed back to you. No follow-up needed.

Step 4 of 4

Review &
submit.

The final screen shows a summary of your items. When you're ready, click Submit & Send. Here's what happens next:

Your checklist is saved to the database
Status set to "Awaiting Advisor" — visible in the Admin Portal.
Email sent to the certification team
They receive your completed items for reference, all your uploaded documents, and a secure "Complete Your Portion" button.
You receive a copy CC'd
Your email is automatically CC'd on the Stage 1 submission email.
Certification team completes Stage 2
They check their items via the link or the Admin Portal.
Final PDF emailed back to you
A completed PDF of the full checklist is automatically generated and sent to your inbox. No follow-up needed.
